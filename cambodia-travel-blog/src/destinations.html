When to Go

Cambodia Through the Seasons

NOV — FEB
Dry

Cool and clear. Peak season. Best for temples and travel.

MAR — MAY
Hot

Very hot and dry. Great value. Best for coast.

JUN — SEP
Wet

Green and lush. Fewer crowds. Tonle Sap fills beautifully.

OCT — NOV
Mild

Shoulder season. Best compromise of weather and value.

Getting There & Around

Practical Information

By Air

Phnom Penh (PNH) and Siem Reap (SAI) are main entry points. Direct flights from Bangkok, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Hong Kong. Budget airlines including AirAsia and Scoot serve the region well.

By Land

Land borders with Thailand (Poipet), Vietnam (Moc Bai), and Laos. Buses from Bangkok, Ho Chi Minh City, and Vientiane. Overland travel is slow but rewarding.

Within Cambodia

Air-conditioned buses connect major cities reliably. Tuk-tuks for short distances. Motorbike rentals from $5–$10/day. Grab works in Phnom Penh and Siem Reap.
